Visiting Cape Clear Island Museum

Cape Clear Island Museum, Cape Clear Island, Baltimore, Co. Cork, Ireland is open daily (12.00 to 16.000) in summertime only from June to early September annually. However, arrangements can be made for groups to visit the centre at other times (weather permitting) provided an advance arrangement/booking is made with Comharchumann Chléire/ Cape Clear Island Co –Op.
Information Bureau: cccteo@iol.ie
Tel. 353 (0)2839119

O Driscoll International Memorial Archive

www.odriscoll.ie 
Email: odriscoll@gmail.com
As the collection and archiving of data relating to the worldwide O Driscoll family is a work in progress, the O Driscoll Collection is not yet available for consultation by the public. However, the compiler of the archive Dr. Éamon Lankford may be able to advise on what it contains and provide assistance if details of a person’s area of study or interest is forwarded to: odiscollarchive@gmail.com

Cape Clear Island Archive

As the collection and archiving of data relating to aspects of Cape Clear island history, culture and heritage is a work in progress, the Éamon Lankford Cape Clear Collection is not yet ready for consultation by the public. However, the compiler of both the Cape Clear Island Archive and the O Driscoll International Memorial Archive will be able to advise on what the archives contain and perhaps may be able to provide assistance if details of a persons’ area of study or interest is forwarded by email to: capecleararchive@gmail.com

Cork County Library

Cork County Library has a comprehensive collection of local history sources (including Cape Clear Island) at the Local Studies Unit, Cork County Library, Carrigrohane Road, Cork, Ireland.
Email: corkcountylibrary@corkcoco.ie
Tel. 353 (0)21 4546499
